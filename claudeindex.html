The Game

A Living World, Dealt Card by Card

Ostentation is a deck-building fantasy role-playing card game where strategy and story are inseparable. Build your champion, expand your deck, and venture into a world where every decision leaves a mark — on the realm, and on you.

Four rival factions. Over two hundred unique cards. A ten-chapter campaign where the world remembers what you've done. Play alone, cooperate with allies, or face off in asymmetric faction warfare.

This is not a game you merely play. It is a game that plays you back.

The Arcana

Six Pillars of the Craft

Every system in Ostentation is designed to serve both strategy and story — mechanics that feel like magic, and magic that feels like a decision.

ᚨ

Living Deck Craft

Your deck evolves across the campaign itself. Cards earned, stolen, and discovered permanently join your arsenal. No two playthroughs are ever the same hand.

ᚱ

Narrative Consequence

Every choice echoes forward. Enemies spared may become allies. Dungeons plundered may seal paths ahead. The world bends and remembers.

ᚦ

Faction Warfare

Four factions — each with unique mechanics, card pools, and narrative arcs. Play cooperative campaigns or clash in asymmetric faction PvP.

ᚷ

The Fortune System

Dice influence draws, negotiations, and hidden card effects. Luck is not randomness — it is a resource. Spend it with intention or lose it to fate.

ᛏ

Campaign Mode

Ten interconnected chapters. Branching story nodes. A persistent world map. A finale shaped entirely by the weight of decisions made along the way.

ᛟ

Solo & Co-op Ready

Every mode is built from the ground up, not retrofitted. Solo campaigns run as deep as four-player co-op, with a full dedicated AI rival system.
